About Me

My name is Merveilles Katumba Biende, and I come from the Democratic Republic of Congo. I'm 31 years old and currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at the University at Buffalo. I've also completed some courses at Erie Community College as part of my academic journey.

Before this, I studied Electrical and Computer Engineering in South Africa and lived there for 10 years. During my time in South Africa, I had the opportunity to explore vibrant cities like Cape Town, Johannesburg, Durban, and Port Elizabeth.

In addition to my travels in South Africa, I've also visited Antananarivo in Madagascar. My passion for exploration and learning has been fueled by my experiences as a missionary, where I had the chance to volunteer and travel extensively.

Currently, I reside in the United States, specifically in New York state, in the city of Buffalo, where I continue to pursue my academic and personal growth.
